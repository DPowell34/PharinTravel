About Pharin Walker

Owner & Travel Agent · Destined 4 Destinations LLC

Hi, I'm Pharin Walker

Owner & Travel Agent · Destined 4 Destinations

Travel isn't just about visiting new places — it's about creating unforgettable memories that last a lifetime. That's why I founded Destined 4 Destinations: to help individuals, couples, families, and groups experience the world with confidence, ease, and excitement.

Since launching my agency in 2018, I've had the privilege of helping travelers plan everything from dream honeymoons and destination weddings to Disney vacations, luxury all-inclusive resorts, cruises, family reunions, birthday celebrations, and bucket-list adventures around the world.

When you work with me, you're not just booking a vacation — you gain a dedicated travel advisor who is committed to making every detail seamless.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 1, 2024  ·  Destined4Destinations LLC

1. Booking & Payment

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation. A deposit is required at the time of booking to hold your reservation. Final payment is due by the date specified on your invoice. Destined 4 Destinations LLC reserves the right to cancel any reservation for which final payment has not been received by the due date. Payments can be made via credit card, debit card, Zelle, or other approved methods.

2. Research & Design (D&B) Fee

A non-refundable Research & Design (D&B) fee may apply to custom itinerary planning and consultation services. This fee covers the time and expertise invested in researching and designing a personalized travel experience for you. This fee is separate from any travel supplier costs and will be disclosed prior to booking.

3. Cancellations & Refunds

Cancellation policies vary by travel supplier and are subject to the terms set by airlines, cruise lines, resorts, and tour operators. All cancellations must be submitted in writing to Destined4Destinations@gmail.com. Deposits are generally non-refundable unless otherwise stated. Any applicable refunds will be processed according to the individual supplier's cancellation policy. We strongly recommend purchasing travel protection to safeguard your investment.

4. Changes & Modifications

Any changes to an existing reservation (dates, destinations, traveler names, etc.) are subject to availability and may incur change fees from the travel supplier as well as a modification service fee. Destined 4 Destinations LLC will make every effort to accommodate requested changes but cannot guarantee availability.

5. Travel Insurance

Destined 4 Destinations LLC strongly recommends that all travelers purchase comprehensive travel insurance, including trip cancellation, medical, and emergency evacuation coverage. Travel insurance options will be offered at the time of booking. Declining travel insurance is done at the traveler's own risk. Destined 4 Destinations LLC is not responsible for losses that could have been covered by travel insurance.

6. Passports, Visas & Travel Documents

It is the sole responsibility of each traveler to ensure they hold valid travel documents required for their destination, including passports, visas, and any health-related requirements. Passports must generally be valid for at least six (6) months beyond the travel return date. Destined 4 Destinations LLC is not responsible for any traveler being denied boarding or entry due to improper or expired documentation.

7. Limitation of Liability

Destined 4 Destinations LLC acts as an agent for airlines, cruise lines, hotels, tour operators, and other travel suppliers. We are not responsible for any injury, damage, loss, accident, delay, or irregularity caused by defects of any vehicle or vessel, or the acts or defaults of any company or person engaged in providing services included in the travel package. Our liability is limited to the amount paid to us for the specific service in question.

8. Force Majeure

Destined 4 Destinations LLC shall not be held liable for any failure to perform obligations where such failure results from causes beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to: acts of God, natural disasters, pandemics, government travel restrictions, war, terrorism, civil unrest, strikes, or any other event outside our control. In such cases, we will work with suppliers to obtain the best possible outcome for our clients.

9. Privacy Policy

Personal information collected through this website and during the booking process is used solely for the purpose of processing your travel reservations and communicating relevant travel information. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your travel bookings. Your information is stored securely and handled with the utmost confidentiality.
